# Governance Changelog

## What This Is
This changelog is the decision log for the Content Bible.
Use it whenever you change rules that affect generation, QA, compliance, scheduling, or reporting.

## Why It Exists
- Keeps strategy changes auditable over time.
- Prevents silent drift in tone/quality/compliance standards.
- Lets you roll back bad rule changes quickly.

## Who Uses It
- Human operator: records approvals, overrides, and policy updates.
- AI automation: appends entries when updating content rules or linter behavior.

## When To Create An Entry
- Updated brand voice or banned phrases.
- New visual preset or major prompt policy change.
- Compliance rule change (affiliate/legal/consent).
- Hook/intent system changes.
- Output format or scheduling rule changes.
